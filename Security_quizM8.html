Welcome

Thank you for making it this far! Now it is time for a quick review and then a test to test your knowledge.

Cybersecurity is much more than protecting computers. It protects people, businesses, governments, and critical services that we rely on every day.

Many cyberattacks begin with simple human mistakes such as clicking a malicious email, using weak passwords, or failing to update software. By understanding common threats and following security best practices, everyone can contribute to a safer digital environment.

Learning Objective

Understand why cybersecurity matters and recognize the role each person plays in protecting information and systems.
